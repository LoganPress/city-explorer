Explore the City of St. Louis

Welcome! This website is an experiential way to interact with and explore the city of St. Louis. We have compiled various data on the neighborhoods of St. Louis City, which are presented here. Here is how to use this site:
- To examine a specific category of data, click on the associated button near the top of the page.
- To get more information on a category, hover over the question mark next to the category's name.
- Click on a neighborhood in the map to focus it in the line chart on the righthand side of the page
- Click on a neighborhood in the map, or in the list of selected neighborhoods, to remove it from the selection
- To see a demo of the tool in use, click the "Screencast" button
To bring up this menu again, press the "Help" button.
